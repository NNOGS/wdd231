My Project Site Plan

NNOGS African Tech Hubs & Startups

This name represents a platform focused on African tech hubs and startups, helping beginners learn about technology, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

Optional domain: nnogsafricantechhub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to inform users about startups and tech hubs, helping aspiring entrepreneurs, investors, and tech enthusiasts discover opportunities and connect with the African tech community.

Scenarios

  • Where is the NNOGS tech hub located in Enugu?
  • How can I join a tech hub or startup community in Enugu?

Color Scheme

  • #2a7ae2 – Headings and primary accents
  • #f9f9f9 – Background color
  • #333333 – Body text

Typography

  • Montserrat – Headings (H1, H2, H3)
  • Roboto – Body text and paragraphs

Additional fonts may be used for special sections as needed.
